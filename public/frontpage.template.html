What is DriveSense?

Using data captured from GPS and your smartphone’s internal sensors, DriveSense can analyze and help to improve your driving habits, keeping you safe, protected, and well-informed whenever you get behind the wheel. The easy-to-use smartphone app stores a history of all trip information, as well as useful metrics like speed, acceleration, and braking.

Whether you’re looking to improve your personal road safety, keep track of young drivers, or monitor a fleet of vehicles in real-time, DriveSense has you covered.

Sophisticated Technology, Simple Interface

Unlike other products that record trip information and driving behavior, DriveSense does not require installation of any outside hardware or peripherals. Instead, it harnesses the GPS capabilities and internal motion sensors already included in most smartphones. DriveSense captures this data as the drive is occurring, and uses it to paint a complete, detailed picture of your trip, storing it in the cloud for easy access from any internet-enabled device.

While the technology and design that propels DriveSense is complex, its operation and user experience is far from it. Featuring a straightforward, uncluttered interface, the mobile app uses only a single button to begin and end the capture of trip data, and an easy-to-navigate list of past trips when reviewing from any device with an internet connection.
